Patrick Murphy

Vice President, Business Development

Patrick oversees the cultivation of business relationships at CREF. He’s responsible for leading the team to new opportunities, nurturing existing partnerships, and is integrally involved with CREF’s market share growth. Patrick’s client-driven with a focus on health care operator and physician relationships. His background is in the health care market, having overseen Steward Health Care System’s real estate portfolio and capital project management program. Patrick holds a bachelor of science in civil engineering from Syracuse University and has been a key player in real estate development for 20 years.
